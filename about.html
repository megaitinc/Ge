15+ Years of Excellence in Home Remodeling

GE Construction Group was founded in 2008 with a simple mission: to deliver exceptional craftsmanship and outstanding customer service to homeowners in Gaithersburg and throughout Montgomery County.

What started as a small family-owned business has grown into a trusted name in the local construction industry, known for our attention to detail, transparent communication, and commitment to quality.

Our team of skilled professionals brings together decades of combined experience in all aspects of home construction and remodeling. We take pride in our work and stand behind every project we complete.
